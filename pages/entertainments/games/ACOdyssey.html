Summery:

“ "Assassin's Creed Odyssey" is an action role-playing video game developed by Ubisoft Quebec and published by Ubisoft. It was released on October 5, 2018, for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One.

The game is set in ancient Greece during the Peloponnesian War between Athens and Sparta. The player takes on the role of either Kassandra or Alexios, a mercenary who is descended from the Spartan king Leonidas I. The story follows their journey as they uncover their family's past and become embroiled in the conflict between Athens and Sparta, as well as the secretive war between the Assassins and the Templars.

The game features an open world environment that allows players to explore ancient Greece, complete quests, and engage in combat with both human and mythical enemies. It also introduces new RPG elements to the series, such as dialogue choices and branching storylines that can affect the game's outcome.

Overall, Assassin's Creed Odyssey received positive reviews from critics and was praised for its expansive open world, engaging story, and improved gameplay mechanics. „
