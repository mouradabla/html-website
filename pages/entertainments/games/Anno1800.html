Summery:

“ "Anno 1800" is a city-building and economic simulation game developed by Blue Byte and published by Ubisoft for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X/S. The game was released in April 2019 and is set in the 19th century, during the Industrial Revolution.

In Anno 1800, players take on the role of a colonial leader and build and manage a city, while also engaging in trade and diplomacy with other players or computer-controlled factions. The game features a complex economy system, which includes various resources that need to be managed, such as food, raw materials, and luxury goods.

Players can also explore the game's world, which is divided into different regions with their own unique resources and challenges. In addition, the game includes a story-driven campaign mode, as well as a sandbox mode, where players can build and manage their city without any specific goals or restrictions.

Anno 1800 received positive reviews from critics and players alike for its detailed gameplay mechanics, immersive atmosphere, and stunning graphics. It is considered one of the best city-building games of recent years and is a popular choice among fans of the genre. „
