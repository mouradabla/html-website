Summery:

“ "A Beautiful Mind" is a 2001 biographical drama film directed by Ron Howard, based on the life of John Nash, a Nobel laureate in Economics. The film stars Russell Crowe as John Nash and Jennifer Connelly as his wife, Alicia Nash. The film depicts Nash's life from his early days as a graduate student at Princeton University, where he developed his mathematical theories, to his struggles with schizophrenia later in life. Despite his mental illness, Nash made significant contributions to game theory, which earned him the Nobel Prize in Economics in 1994.

The film received critical acclaim and won four Academy Awards, including Best Picture, Best Director, Best Supporting Actress for Connelly, and Best Adapted Screenplay.

The film was praised for its sensitive portrayal of mental illness and its ability to bring to life the complexities of Nash's mathematical work „
