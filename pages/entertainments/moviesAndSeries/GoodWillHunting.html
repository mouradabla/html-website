Summery:

“ "Good Will Hunting" is a 1997 American drama film directed by Gus Van Sant and starring Matt Damon, Ben Affleck, and Robin Williams. The movie tells the story of Will Hunting (played by Damon), a young man from South Boston who has a genius-level intellect but works as a janitor at MIT.

The film follows Will as he meets and forms a close friendship with Sean Maguire (played by Williams), a therapist who helps him confront his past traumas and overcome his emotional barriers. Along the way, Will also navigates his romantic relationship with Skylar (played by Minnie Driver) and faces a difficult decision about his future.

"Good Will Hunting" was critically acclaimed and won two Academy Awards, including Best Original Screenplay for Damon and Affleck, who wrote the script. The movie is considered a classic of the 1990s and is remembered for its powerful performances and emotional storytelling. „
